Short bio

I am a PHD student at Hubert Curien Laboratory, University Jean Monnet, Saint-Étienne in the Image Science and Computer Vision team. I am studying denoising of cyclostationary signals using Machine Learning (and more precisely Deep Learning) tools under the supervision of Olivier Alata, Christophe Ducottet and Fabien Momey.

Research Interests:

Denoising, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Cyclostationary signals
